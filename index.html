What “Pro-active” Means Here

Pro-active describes behaviour.
An attitude. A particular headspace.

  • internally directed
  • self-accountable
  • consistent without supervision
  • dependable
  • action is your currency
  • willing to step up for those who support you
  • capable of operating within defined agreements

This program recognises sustained execution.

This is not about potential.

You don’t need to be loud.
You don’t need to perform.
You don’t need a huge following.

Selection is based on discipline, consistency, and visible real-world doing.

What Support Looks Like

Support is allocated in defined Components.

  • gym or training membership
  • competition or event entry
  • travel and accommodation
  • production costs
  • content development support

Each Component carries agreed activation expectations.
